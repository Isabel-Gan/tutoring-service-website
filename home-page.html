ABOUT US

"Symbolically, [elm trees] represent new beginnings. Old-growth elms are traditionally found on college campuses ... [T]hese trees represent intuition and inner strength."

Who we are

We are a group of rising sophomores at Carnegie Mellon University studying a variety of majors, including computer science, mechanical engineering, and business.

Our Passion

Education. As college students who absolutely love our university and the opportunities that CMU provides us, we understand that education is the key to success and want to support others on their path.

Support

With the transition to online learning, we've realized it's difficult to maintain the same quality of motivation and education. We want to ensure that every student is supported and encouraged to keep learning no matter what.

Design

We've designed a platform that encourages students to keep learn about anything they want, where they want. If you're struggling, we'll provide opportunities. If you want to help, you can do it here. If you're unmotivated, look no further.
